Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2025

Data Collection

TranscribeEN does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, audio files, video files, or transcripts.

On-Device Processing

All transcription happens on your device. Your files never leave your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

Local Storage

  • Files you select are stored locally on your device
  • Transcripts are saved in your app's local database
  • No data is uploaded to servers or shared with third parties

Permissions

File Access: To select and process audio/video files

Storage: To save transcripts locally on your device

AI Model

The app downloads an AI model from Hugging Face (one-time download). After download, the app works completely offline.

No Tracking

We do not use analytics, tracking, or third-party services.
