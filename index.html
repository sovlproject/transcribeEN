Frequently Asked Questions

How do I transcribe audio or video? +

1. Tap "Select New File" on the home screen
2. Choose "Photo Library" or "Files"
3. Select your audio or video file
4. The app will automatically detect the language and transcribe it to English
5. Watch or listen while reading the synchronized transcript

How do I save a transcript? +

After transcription is complete, tap the "Save to Library" button. Your transcript will be saved with the original media file, language information, and timestamp data. Access all your saved transcripts in the Library tab.

How do I export transcripts? +

1. Tap the "Export" button in the transcript view
2. Choose your format:
‚Ä¢ Transcript (.txt) - Plain text with timestamps
‚Ä¢ Subtitles (.srt) - Standard subtitle format for videos
3. Share via Messages, Mail, Files, or any app
The files will be saved to your chosen location.

What languages are supported? +

TranscribeEN supports 90+ languages including Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Chinese (Mandarin & Cantonese), Hindi, Arabic, Portuguese, Russian, Italian, Dutch, Turkish, and many more. The app automatically detects the source language and translates it to English.

What file formats are supported? +

Audio: MP3, M4A, WAV, AAC
Video: MP4, MOV, M4V, AVI, MKV
You can select files from your Photo Library or Files app.

Does this require an internet connection? +

No! After the initial model download (one-time, ~40-500MB depending on your device), TranscribeEN works completely offline. All AI processing happens on your device for maximum privacy and speed. No cloud uploads, no data sharing.

How accurate are the transcriptions? +

TranscribeEN uses OpenAI's Whisper model, achieving 85-95% accuracy depending on audio quality, accents, and background noise. For best results, use clear audio with minimal background noise. The app works best with spoken language like conversations, lectures, podcasts, and interviews.

Is my data private and secure? +

Absolutely! TranscribeEN processes everything on your device using on-device AI. Your audio, video, and transcripts never leave your iPhone or iPad. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. No accounts required, no cloud storage, complete privacy.
